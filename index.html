Who I Am

Validation Engineer. Product Builder.

By Day — MedTech

I work as a Validation Engineer in the medical technology industry — ensuring devices meet rigorous quality, safety, and regulatory standards. This background shapes how I build: methodically, reliably, and with zero tolerance for cutting corners.

By Passion — Products

Vystra is where I channel my engineering mindset into building software products. Not just side projects — real, shipped products that solve problems for real users. The same discipline I apply to validation, I apply to code.

The Philosophy

Find a real problem. Build a focused solution. Ship it. Iterate. No buzzwords, no bloat — just well-engineered products for underserved markets. Construction was first. More domains are coming.
